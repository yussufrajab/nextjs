## 1. Project Summary

### 1.1 Project Background

The Civil Service Commission of Zanzibar manages HR operations for over 50,000 government employees across 85+ institutions. Prior to CSMS, all HR processes were manual and paper-based, resulting in:

- Long processing times (average 15 days per request)
- High administrative overhead
- Lack of transparency and tracking
- Data inconsistencies and errors
- Difficulty in generating reports
- Limited audit trail
- Storage and archiving challenges

**Business Need:** A modern, digital HR management system to automate processes, improve efficiency, and enhance service delivery.

### 1.2 Project Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**

1. Automate all HR request workflows (9 types)
2. Centralize employee data management
3. Implement role-based access control (9 roles)
4. Enable real-time status tracking
5. Provide comprehensive reporting and analytics
6. Establish complete audit trail
7. Integrate document management
8. Support bilingual operations (English/Swahili)
